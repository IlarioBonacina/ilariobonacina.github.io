I’m a postdoc in the Theoretical Computer Science Group at the School of Computer Science and Communication (CSC) at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. I’m hosted by Jakob Nordström.

Previously I was a PhD student at the Computer Science Department at Sapienza University of Rome under the supervision of Nicola Galesi.

I’m interested in Computational Complexity, Mathematical Logic and in particular in Proof Complexity.
