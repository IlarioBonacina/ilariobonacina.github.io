  12. (ll)  
    ​

    {p∣φp⁢ es exhaustiva y total}\{p\ \mid\ \varphi_{p}\mbox{ es exhaustiva y total}\}.

  13. (mm)

  14. (nn)

    {p∣φp⁢ es total y estríctamente decreciente}\{p\ \mid\ \varphi_{p}\mbox{ es total y estríctamente decreciente}\}.

  15. (oo)  
    ​

    {p∣φp⁢ es inyectiva parcial}\{p\ \mid\ \varphi_{p}\mbox{ es inyectiva parcial}\}.

Exercici 7.2.

Clasifica como decidibles, no decidibles pero semidecidibles, o no semidecidibles, los siguientes conjuntos.

  1. (aa)  
    ​

    {⟨p,q⟩∣∀z:((Mp(z)↓∧Mq(z)↑)∨(Mp(z)↑∧Mq(z)↓))}\{\langle p,q\rangle\ \mid\ \forall z:((M_{p}(z)\downarrow\wedge M_{q}(z)% \uparrow)\vee(M_{p}(z)\uparrow\wedge M_{q}(z)\downarrow))\}.

  2. (bb)  
    ​

    {⟨p,z⟩∣∃y:Mp⁢(y)=z}\{\langle p,z\rangle\ \mid\ \exists y:M_{p}(y)=z\}.

  3. (cc)  
    ​

    {⟨p,z⟩∣∃y:Mp⁢(y)≠z}\{\langle p,z\rangle\ \mid\ \exists y:M_{p}(y)\not=z\}.

  4. (dd)  
    ​

    {p∣ℒp⁢ es incontextual}\{p\ \mid\ {\mathcal{L}}_{p}\mbox{ es incontextual}\}.

  5. (ee)  
    ​

    {p∣ℒp⁢ no es incontextual}\{p\ \mid\ {\mathcal{L}}_{p}\mbox{ no es incontextual}\}.

  6. (ff)  
    ​

    {p∣𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φp)∈𝙳𝚎𝚌}\{p\ \mid\ {\tt Dom}(\varphi_{p})\in{\tt Dec}\}.

  7. (gg)  
    ​

    {p∣𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φp)∉𝙳𝚎𝚌}\{p\ \mid\ {\tt Dom}(\varphi_{p})\not\in{\tt Dec}\}.

  8. (hh)  
    ​

    {p∣𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φp)∉𝚜𝚎𝚖𝚒−𝙳𝚎𝚌}\{p\ \mid\ {\tt Dom}(\varphi_{p})\not\in{\tt semi-Dec}\}.

  9. (ii)  
    ​

    {p∣𝙸𝚖⁢(φp)∈𝙳𝚎𝚌}\{p\ \mid\ {\tt Im}(\varphi_{p})\in{\tt Dec}\}.

  10. (jj)  
    ​

    {p∣𝙸𝚖⁢(φp)∉𝙳𝚎𝚌}\{p\ \mid\ {\tt Im}(\varphi_{p})\not\in{\tt Dec}\}.

  11. (kk)  
    ​

    {p∣𝙸𝚖⁢(φp)∈𝚜𝚎𝚖𝚒−𝙳𝚎𝚌}\{p\ \mid\ {\tt Im}(\varphi_{p})\in{\tt semi-Dec}\}.

  12. (ll)  
    ​

    {p∣𝙸𝚖⁢(φp)∉𝚜𝚎𝚖𝚒−𝙳𝚎𝚌}\{p\ \mid\ {\tt Im}(\varphi_{p})\not\in{\tt semi-Dec}\}.

  13. (mm)  
    ​

    {p∣p≤100∧𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φp)∈𝙳𝚎𝚌}\{p\ \mid\ p\leq 100\wedge{\tt Dom}(\varphi_{p})\in{\tt Dec}\}.

  14. (nn)  
    ​

    {p∣p≥100∧𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φp)∈𝚜𝚎𝚖𝚒−𝙳𝚎𝚌}\{p\ \mid\ p\geq 100\wedge{\tt Dom}(\varphi_{p})\in{\tt semi-Dec}\}.

  15. (oo)  
    ​

    {p∣∀y>p:φy⁢ es biyectiva}\{p\ \mid\ \forall y>p:\varphi_{y}\mbox{ es biyectiva}\}.

  16. (pp)  
    ​

    {p∣∀y<p:φy⁢ es biyectiva}\{p\ \mid\ \forall y<p:\varphi_{y}\mbox{ es biyectiva}\}.

  17. (qq)  
    ​

    {p∣∃y>p:φy⁢ es biyectiva}\{p\ \mid\ \exists y>p:\varphi_{y}\mbox{ es biyectiva}\}.

  18. (rr)  
    ​

    {p∣∃y<p:φy⁢ es biyectiva}\{p\ \mid\ \exists y<p:\varphi_{y}\mbox{ es biyectiva}\}.

  19. (ss)  
    ​

    {p∣∃y:𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φp)⊆𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φy)}\{p\ \mid\ \exists y:{\tt Dom}(\varphi_{p})\subseteq{\tt Dom}(\varphi_{y})\}.

  20. (tt)  
    ​

    {p∣∃y:𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φp)⊇𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φy)}\{p\ \mid\ \exists y:{\tt Dom}(\varphi_{p})\supseteq{\tt Dom}(\varphi_{y})\}.

  21. (uu)  
    ​

    {p∣𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φp)⊆2˙}\{p\ \mid\ {\tt Dom}(\varphi_{p})\subseteq\dot{2}\}.

  22. (vv)  
    ​

    {p∣𝙳𝚘𝚖⁢(φp)⊇2˙}\{p\ \mid\ {\tt Dom}(\varphi_{p})\supseteq\dot{2}\}.

Exercici 7.9.

La función característica de un conjunto CC se define como:

χC⁢(x)={1si ⁢x∈C0si ⁢x∉C\chi_{C}(x)=\left\{\begin{array}[]{rcl}1&&\mbox{si }x\in C\\ 0&&\mbox{si }x\not\in C\\ \end{array}\right.

Demuestra que CC es decidible si y solo si su función característica χC\chi_{C} es computable.
