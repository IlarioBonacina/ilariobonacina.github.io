  1. (aa)  
    ​

    LkL_{k}.

  2. (bb)  
    ​

    ⋃k≥1Lk\bigcup_{k\geq 1}L_{k}.

  3. (cc)  
    ​

    {w⁢#⁢w|w∈Lk}\{w\#w|w\in L_{k}\}.

  4. (dd)  
    ​

    {1⁢w⁢#⁢1⁢w|1⁢w∈Lk}\{1w\#1w|1w\in L_{k}\}.

  5. (ee)  
    ​

    {1⁢w⁢#⁢1⁢w|w∈Lk}\{1w\#1w|w\in L_{k}\}.

  6. (ff)  
    ​

    {w⁢#⁢w|w∈Lk∧|w|≤𝚟𝚊𝚕𝚘𝚛2⁢(w)}\{w\#w|w\in L_{k}\;\wedge\;|w|\leq{\tt valor}_{2}(w)\}.

  7. (gg)  
    ​

    {w⁢#⁢w|1⁢w∈Lk}\{w\#w|1w\in L_{k}\}.

  8. (hh)  
    ​

    {w1⁢#⁢w2|w1,w2∈Lk∧𝚟𝚊𝚕𝚘𝚛2⁢(w1)=𝚟𝚊𝚕𝚘𝚛2⁢(w2)}\{w_{1}\#w_{2}|w_{1},w_{2}\in L_{k}\wedge{\tt valor}_{2}(w_{1})={\tt valor}_{2% }(w_{2})\}.

  9. (ii)  
    ​

    {w1⁢#⁢w2|∃k:(w1,w2∈Lk∧𝚟𝚊𝚕𝚘𝚛2⁢(w1)=𝚟𝚊𝚕𝚘𝚛2⁢(w2))}\{w_{1}\#w_{2}|\exists k:(w_{1},w_{2}\in L_{k}\;\wedge\;{\tt valor}_{2}(w_{1})% ={\tt valor}_{2}(w_{2}))\}.

  1. (aa)

  2. (bb)

  3. (cc)

  4. (dd)
